<p><p>The floor-mounted fan coil market is categorized into surface-mounted and concealed installations. Surface-mounted fan coils are visible and typically installed along walls, providing easy access for maintenance and operation. In contrast, concealed installations integrate the fan coil units into the building's structure, such as underfloor spaces or within ceiling cavities, offering a more aesthetically pleasing appearance. Both types serve to efficiently regulate indoor climate by utilizing chilled water or hot water to condition air, enhancing comfort in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
