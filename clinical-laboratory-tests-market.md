<p><p>The clinical laboratory tests market encompasses various essential diagnostic tools used to assess health. The Complete Blood Count (CBC) evaluates overall health by measuring different blood components. The Basic Metabolic Panel (BMP) assesses glucose, electrolytes, and kidney function. The Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) adds liver function tests to the BMP. The Lipid Panel measures cholesterol levels, vital for cardiovascular health. HbA1c tests monitor long-term blood sugar control for diabetes management. Together, these tests are integral to preventive care and disease management.</p></p>
