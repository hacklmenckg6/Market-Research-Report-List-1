<p><strong>The Industrial Pressure Casting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-Pressure Casting System</li><li>High-Pressure Casting System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial pressure casting system market consists of low-pressure and high-pressure casting systems, each serving distinct applications. Low-pressure casting systems utilize low-pressure gas to push molten metal into molds, offering enhanced control and reducing defects in large, thin-walled components, commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries. Conversely, high-pressure casting systems inject molten metal at high pressure, enabling rapid production of complex shapes with tight tolerances, widely utilized in consumer electronics and precision engineering sectors. Both systems contribute significantly to manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
