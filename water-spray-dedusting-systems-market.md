<p><p>Water spray dedusting systems are designed to control dust in various industrial environments. The market can be divided into several types: </p><p>1. **Gravity Systems** rely on the natural flow of water and dust to settle, effectively reducing airborne particles.</p><p>2. **Whirlwind Systems** use high-velocity water sprays to create a vortex, capturing dust through centrifugal force.</p><p>3. **Bubble Systems** generate air bubbles in water, which then absorb and trap dust particles.</p><p>4. **Others** encompass innovative or hybrid technologies that combine these principles for enhanced dust suppression.</p></p>
