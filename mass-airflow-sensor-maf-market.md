<p><p>The Mass Airflow Sensor (MAF) market includes several types of sensors that measure the amount of air entering an engine for optimal fuel injection. Vane Air Flow Sensors use a mechanical vane to gauge airflow, while Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensors detect vortices created by fluid flow. Hot Wire Air Flow Sensors measure the cooling effect of air on a heated wire, and Hot Film Air Flow Sensors utilize a thin film to sense airflow, each offering unique advantages in precision and response time.</p></p>
