<p><ul><li>Gross Working Capital (GWC)</li><li>Net Working Capital (NWC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Working Capital Management is crucial for maintaining a firm's liquidity and operational efficiency. Market types include Gross Working Capital (GWC) and Net Working Capital (NWC). GWC refers to the total investment in current assets, focusing on the assets available to support day-to-day operations. In contrast, NWC considers the difference between current assets and current liabilities, highlighting the actual liquidity available to the business. Effective management of both aspects ensures a healthy cash flow and supports growth initiatives.</p></p>
