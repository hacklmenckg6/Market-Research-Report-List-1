<p><strong>The Container Stacking Cranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rubber Tyred Gantry Cranes (RTG)</li><li>Rail Mounted Gantry Crane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The container stacking crane market primarily comprises two types: Rubber Tyred Gantry (RTG) cranes and Rail Mounted Gantry (RMG) cranes. RTGs are versatile, mobile cranes equipped with rubber tires, allowing for flexibility in container yards, while RMGs are fixed structures that run on rails, providing enhanced stability and high-density stacking capabilities. Both types are essential for optimizing container handling efficiency in ports and terminals, with RTGs often preferred for their maneuverability, and RMGs valued for their capacity and speed.</p></p>
