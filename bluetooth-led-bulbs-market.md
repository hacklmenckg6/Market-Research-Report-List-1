<p><p>The Bluetooth LED bulbs market includes various types of lighting technologies. Compact Fluorescent Lights (CFL) are energy-efficient alternatives to incandescent bulbs but are less versatile with Bluetooth capabilities. High-Intensity Discharge Lamps (HID) provide intense illumination, mainly used in commercial settings. Fluorescent Lamps (FL) are common in large spaces but lack modern control features. Light Emitting Diodes (LED) are the preferred choice, offering smart connectivity, energy efficiency, and long lifespans. The "Others" category includes niche products and emerging technologies within smart lighting.</p></p>
