<p><p>The ICU invasive ventilators market is primarily segmented into two types: mechanical ventilators and electric turbo ventilators. Mechanical ventilators use positive pressure to assist or control a patient's breathing, providing consistent support for those with respiratory failure. In contrast, electric turbo ventilators utilize advanced technology to deliver air more efficiently, often featuring integrated sensors for precise monitoring. Both types play critical roles in intensive care, catering to varying patient needs and supporting respiratory management in critical conditions.</p></p>
