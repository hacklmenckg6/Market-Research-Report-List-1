<p><p>The Smart Machine Vision System market is primarily segmented into Line Scan Camera Systems and Area Scan Camera Systems. Line Scan Cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for high-speed applications like web inspection and quality control in production lines. In contrast, Area Scan Cameras capture full frames or images at once, suited for stationary inspection tasks such as barcode reading and product verification. Both systems enhance automation, quality assurance, and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
